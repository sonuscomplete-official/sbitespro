Corporate Wellness Packages

Busselton, Dunsborough and the South West of WA

Corporate Wellness Package Benefits

Workplace benefits may include:

  • reducing stress, anxiety and fatigue
  • strengthening the immune system
  • improving concentration and mental focus
  • increasing productivity and energy levels
  • promoting feelings of calmness and wellbeing.

Looking for a fun, healthy way for staff to de-stress at work?

  • Value add to your next conference, professional development, team building or wellness day.
  • Book a block of regular classes (for example eight weeks) that fit into your work schedule.

KatieYoga currently offers a number of Corporate Wellness Packages that will help promote wellness in your workplace (see below). Alternatively, KatieYoga can tailor classes to meet your needs, whether it’s mindfulness, relaxation, yoga, stress management, wellness or a combination of these, please contact us with your request.

Corporate Wellness Packages

Mindfulness Part 1 - An Introduction to the practice of Mindfulness.

  • Learn how to integrate mindfulness into daily activities to increase productivity and decreases stress.
  • Mindfulness can improve concentration, productivity, resilience and the ability to respond to stress.
  • Mindfulness can help reduce anxiety, depression, stress, physical pain, emotional reactivity and reliance on unhealthy coping strategies.

Mindfulness Part 2 – Woking Mindfully with Sensations, Thoughts and Emotions.

  • Learn how to witness challenging sensations, thoughts and emotions with acceptance and empower yourself through learning strategies to change how you respond to them.

Meditation 101

  • Current research shows that meditation can increase concentration and attention; reduce stress; and improve brain functioning, relationships and happiness.
  • Demystify the practice of meditation and learn how to practice meditation at home.

Yoga Class

  • Create a yoga class to suit your employees’ needs, from strength and fitness to de-stress and relaxation.

Yoga for Weight Loss

  • Learn about Mindfulness as a strategy for moderating eating. Explore mindful eating practices, working mindfully with cravings, appropriate portion size and cultivating self-compassion.

Yoga Ergonomics

  • Learn yoga practices that are designed for improving posture. Tailor the class to suit your employees’ work environment.

Stress Management

  • Explore stress management and develop strategies for responding to stress.

Mental Health in the Workplace

  • Explore mental health in the workplace and strategies to optimise mental health.

Capability Statement

Katie has delivered Corporate Wellness Packages to numerous organisations including the City of Busselton, South West Institute of Technology, Water Corporation, Durack Institute of Technology, Nagle Catholic College, HBF and PMH as well as many other private enterprises.

Katie is a qualified teacher and workplace trainer. She has specialised in adult education and training since 2004, delivering for organisations such as Durack Institute of Technology and Edith Cowan University. She has provided education and training in the corporate environment since 2005. In 2011, Katie coordinated the upskilling of Durack Institute of Technology's lecturing staff (approximately 150 staff members) in Geraldton, Carnarvon and Exmouth to the current Certificate IV in Training and Assessment qualification.
