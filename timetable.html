Style

Enjoy flowing combination of asanas (yoga postures), pranayama (breathing techniques) and relaxation/meditation with Katie Yoga. Each class is specifically designed to help you increase strength, flexibility and balance, and assist you in becoming more mindful and present by uniting the body and mind though the breath.

Beginners: Gentle & Intermediate
A slow paced practice that is suitable for beginners, pregnant women, seniors and people recovering from illness or injury. This practice is also beneficial for experienced yoga students who simply want to return to basics or do a more gentle practice. Modifications are given to individuals to meet their specific needs and props (blankets, blocks and straps) are used to make asanas/yoga postures more accessible for individuals.

Flow: All Levels
An invigorating yoga practice suitable for both beginners and experienced yoga students. Modifications and variations of poses are provided so students can tailor the class to suit their level or needs on that day. Props (blankets, blocks and straps) are used to make asanas/yoga postures more accessible for individuals.

Flow: Strength
An active and dynamic yoga practice that focuses on building strength and stability, suitable for both beginners and experienced yoga students. Modifications and variations of poses are provided so students can tailor the class to suit their level or needs on that day. Props (blankets, blocks and straps) are used to make asanas/yoga postures more accessible for individuals.

Chair Yoga
A slow paced practice that is suitable for beginners, seniors and people recovering from illness or injury. The practie involves a combination of standing postures and seated postures in a chair, it does not include any floor work. Modifications are given to individuals where required.

Restorative Yoga
A restful practice that aims to reduce stress and elicit the relaxation response. The body is supported through the use of props (blocks, bolsters, blankets and straps) and held in relaxing and gently opening postures for long periods of time. The intent of the practice is to truly rest. Perfect for when you’re feeling tired, weak, stressed, unwell or injured.

Yoga on the Busselton Jetty
A flowing yoga practice suitable for beginners and experienced yoga students. Modifications and variations of poses are provided so students can tailor the class to suit their level or needs on that day. Classes are at the end of the Busselton Jetty and the walk out the Jetty takes about 20 to 30 minutes. Limited yoga mats are available to hire for $5 each, please BYO to avoid disappointment. Props will not be provided, please BYO if needed. In inclement weather please check katieyoga Facebook Page for availability. Click here for more details.
